Do You Ship Live Fish?



No, we do not ship or sell live fish for a variety of reasons. Primarily it is because we do not want the fish to endure the stress of shipping.
We can connect you to local fish sellers in your area! Just contact us through our contact for and we will get you set up!

What is The Return Policy?



We offer returns on a case by case basis. If you are unhappy with your purchase, contact us and we will happily review your case and get back with you. We want you to be satisfied with your purchase and we will work hard to resolve the issue.

What if you don't sell a product I need?



Sometimes you need a particular product for your aquarium that we don't sell. No worries! Contact us and let us know what you are looking for. We are always adding new products to our inventory and knowing that are customers are looking for a particular item helps us know what to sell.

In the meantime, we can get you in contact with a company that does sell the product you need so that you do not have to wait!

Are your products safe for people and pets?



Yes! We strive to ensure that all of our products are safe for use around people and pets. We sell products that have been tested to make sure they do no leak toxic chemicals into the water and are free of sharp edges. All of our chemicals sold are safe to be used in an aquarium with fish. Please be careful using chemicals around children and make sure they are stored safely.
